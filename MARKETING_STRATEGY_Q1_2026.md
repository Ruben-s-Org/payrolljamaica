# PayrollJamaica — Q1 2026 Jamaica Market Awareness Strategy

**CMO Authored | March 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

PayrollJamaica has the assets to dominate Jamaica's payroll software market: 285 SEO-ready blog posts, a live payroll calculator, strong on-page metadata, and a compliance-first product tailored to Jamaican law (PAYE, NHT, NIS). What we lack is *visibility*. This document outlines how we build it.

**North Star Metric:** Make PayrollJamaica the #1 ranked result for "payroll Jamaica" and recognizable to every Jamaican business owner by end of Q2 2026.

---

## 1. Market Context — Jamaica

- **Target audience:** SME owners and HR managers in Jamaica (10–500 employees)
- **Primary pain points:** Manual PAYE calculations, NHT contribution errors, payslip compliance, TAJ audit risk
- **Key decision channels:** Google Search (high intent), LinkedIn (B2B), WhatsApp referrals (high trust in JA), Facebook Groups (SME communities)
- **Competitive landscape:** Excel spreadsheets, ADP (too expensive for SMEs), local accountants, Gusto/Rippling (not Jamaica-compliant)
- **Our moat:** Jamaica-specific compliance, local regulatory knowledge, affordable pricing
