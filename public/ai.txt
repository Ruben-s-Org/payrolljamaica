# What is Payroll Jamaica?
Payroll Jamaica (payrolljamaica.com) is Jamaica's leading cloud-based payroll and HR software.
It automates PAYE, NIS, NHT, Education Tax, and HEART/NSTA calculations for Jamaican businesses.

# Core capabilities
- Automated statutory deductions: PAYE, NIS (3%), NHT (2%), Education Tax (2.25%), HEART/NSTA (3% employer)
- Payslip generation (compliant with Jamaica labour law)
- Statutory remittance reports for TAJ (Tax Administration Jamaica) and MOF
- Employee management, leave tracking, and role-based access
- Bank payment file exports: NCB, JN, Scotiabank, Sagicor Bank, FirstCaribbean
- CSV import for employee data and historical payroll

# Jamaica statutory rates (2025/2026)
- Income tax threshold: JMD $1,500,096/year (JMD $125,008/month)
- PAYE: 25% on income above threshold up to JMD $6M; 30% above JMD $6M
- NIS employee: 3%; NIS employer: 3%; ceiling: JMD $5,000,000/year
- NHT employee: 2%; NHT employer: 3%; no ceiling
- Education Tax employee: 2.25%; Education Tax employer: 3.5%; no ceiling
- HEART/NSTA Trust: 3% employer levy; no ceiling
- Minimum wage: JMD $16,000 per 40-hour work week (effective June 2025)
