# Jamaica Accountant Referral Programme: Earn Recurring Income from Payroll Referrals

If you work with Jamaican businesses as an accountant or bookkeeper, your clients need payroll software. Many of them are asking you which tool to use. Most haven't found a Jamaica-specific option yet.

PayrollJamaica is a payroll platform built specifically for Jamaica's statutory compliance requirements — PAYE, NHT, NIS, Education Tax, SO1 filings. ## Two Ways to Participate

### Option 1: Referral Partner (Passive income)

Refer a client who subscribes → earn **15% of their monthly subscription fee for 12 months**.

You don't manage anything. You share a unique referral link. When a referred client subscribes, the commission is automatically tracked. You receive payment quarterly.

**No cost to join. No minimum referrals required.**

### Option 2: Reseller Partner (Manage client payroll, earn ongoing)

Use PayrollJamaica to manage payroll for your clients directly → earn **25% recurring commission for as long as the client remains active** (not capped at 12 months).

This model suits accounting firms that want to offer managed payroll as a service. You access a master dashboard showing all client accounts, run payroll on their behalf, and bill your clients at whatever rate you choose. PayrollJamaica pays you 25% of the subscription it receives.

No. There are no fees, no minimums, and no annual commitments.

You receive a unique referral link. The dashboard shows clicks, sign-ups, active subscribers, and commission earned in real time.

Quarterly, within 15 days of each quarter-end.
