## Payroll Jamaica Launches Jamaica's First Dedicated Payroll Software Platform, Offering Free Trial to All Jamaican Businesses

*Kingston-based platform automates PAYE, NIS, NHT, and Education Tax for Jamaican employers — just in time for the March 31 annual return deadline*

**KINGSTON, JAMAICA — March 2026** — Payroll Jamaica, the island's first payroll software platform built from the ground up for Jamaican statutory compliance, today announced the launch of its flagship product and a free trial offer for Jamaican businesses of all sizes.

The platform automates the calculation, filing, and remittance of all Jamaican payroll deductions — including PAYE, NIS, NHT, Education Tax, and HEART/NSTA levy — and generates SO1/SO2 annual returns required by Tax Administration Jamaica (TAJ) by the March 31 deadline.

### A Jamaican Solution for a Jamaican Problem

Until now, Jamaican employers have relied on international software packages not designed for Jamaican tax law, manual spreadsheets prone to error, or expensive managed payroll services. Payroll Jamaica was designed specifically around Jamaica's statutory framework — TAJ, NIS, NHT, and the Holidays with Pay Act — without requiring employers to adapt generic tools.

"Every Jamaican business owner has felt the stress of March 31," said a spokesperson for Payroll Jamaica. "SO1 forms, NHT schedules, PAYE reconciliations — it should not take a week of manual work. We built Payroll Jamaica so any business owner can run compliant payroll in minutes, not hours."

### Key Features

- **Automatic statutory calculations** — PAYE, NIS, NHT, Education Tax calculated on every pay run
- **Free PAYE calculator** — available at payrolljamaica.com/calculator with no login required
- **Jamaica-specific compliance** — TAJ filing templates, SO1/SO2 annual returns, NIS/NHT schedules
- **Sector-specific tools** — built for hotels, restaurants, construction, healthcare, churches, security companies, and small businesses
- **Payslip generation** — professional, TAJ-compliant payslips in PDF format
- **Free trial** — no credit card required

### March 31 Deadline: 20 Days Away

With Jamaica's tax year-end deadline approaching on March 31, Payroll Jamaica is offering all new users immediate access to file their SO1/SO2 annual returns. Employers who have not yet filed their annual returns can sign up for a free trial and use the platform to generate compliant returns from their payroll data.

Penalties for late or incorrect SO1/SO2 filings can reach J$10,000 per employee. Payroll Jamaica eliminates this risk by generating returns automatically.

### Pricing and Availability

Payroll Jamaica is available online at **payrolljamaica.com**. Pricing starts from J$2,500 per month and scales with the number of employees. A free trial is available with no credit card required.
