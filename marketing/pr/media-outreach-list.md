## Notes on Pitching Jamaica Media

- **Lead with the deadline**: "March 31" is the hook. Every pitch email subject line should include it.
- **Offer interviews readily**: Jamaica editors respond well to accessible spokespeople. Make it easy — offer a phone number and available times upfront.
- **Keep pitches short**: One to three paragraphs max. Attach the press release; don't paste the whole thing in the email.
- **Social media DMs work**: Loop Jamaica and JBDC in particular can be effectively reached via Facebook page DMs if email doesn't get a response.
- **Free = news**: Emphasize the zero-cost, no-signup nature of the tool in every pitch. This is genuinely unusual and newsworthy for the audience.
