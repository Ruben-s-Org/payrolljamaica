## Overview: Hashtag Philosophy

Hashtags are not decoration — they are discovery infrastructure. Our hashtag strategy serves three purposes:

1. **Reach**: Get PayrollJamaica content in front of people who don't yet follow us
2. **Authority**: Be consistently associated with the hashtags that Jamaica business professionals actually search
3. **Community**: Participate in and eventually own the conversation around Jamaica payroll and compliance

Different platforms have different hashtag dynamics. This guide covers each separately.

## Platform-Specific Hashtag Recipes

### LINKEDIN HASHTAG RECIPE
**How many:** 3–5 hashtags maximum (LinkedIn algorithm deprioritizes heavy hashtag use)
**Where:** End of post, after main content
**Format:** Full names, relevant to post

**Standard Educational Post:**
```
#PayrollJamaica #PAYEJamaica #JamaicaHR #JamaicaBusiness #TAJCompliance
```

**Year-End/Deadline Post:**
```
#PayrollJamaica #FiscalYearEnd #JamaicaBusiness #PAYEJamaica #JamaicaHR
```

**Product Post:**

### INSTAGRAM HASHTAG RECIPE
**How many:** 8–15 hashtags (Instagram still rewards hashtag use; 8 is minimum for reach)
**Where:** End of caption, after main content (or in first comment if caption is long)
**Format:** Mix of Tier 1–5

**Standard Instagram Formula (12 hashtags):**
```
#PayrollJamaica [Tier 2: 2-3 topic] [Tier 3: 3-4 audience] [Tier 4: 1-2 niche] [Tier 5: 1 cultural]
```

**Example — Educational Carousel Post:**

#PayrollJamaica #PAYEJamaica #NHTJamaica #JamaicaHR #JamaicanBusiness #SMEJamaica #BusinessOwner #HRManager #TaxJamaica #FiscalYearEnd #JamaicaBusiness #Compliance

### X (TWITTER) HASHTAG RECIPE
**How many:** 1–2 hashtags maximum (X penalizes over-tagging; one strong hashtag beats five weak ones)
**Where:** End of tweet or woven naturally into the text
**Format:** Choose one brand + one topic, or one compound tag

**Standard Formula:**
```
#JamaicaBusiness #PayrollJamaica
```
or
```
#PAYEJamaica
```
(for urgent/news-type tweets, one is enough)

**Breaking regulatory news:**
```
#TAJJamaica
```

**Year-end urgency:**
```
#JamaicaBusiness
```
(paired with URL — the URL is the CTA, not the hashtag)

---

### TIKTOK HASHTAG RECIPE
**How many:** 4–8 hashtags (TikTok uses hashtags for category classification; middle ground works best)
**Where:** In video description
**Format:** Mix viral/broad tags with niche Jamaica tags

**Standard TikTok Formula:**
```
#PayrollJamaica #JamaicaBusiness #LearnOnTikTok #JamaicanEntrepreneur [1-2 topic tags]
```

**Example — Educational Tip Video:**
```
#PayrollJamaica #JamaicaFinance #LearnOnTikTok #BusinessTips #JamaicanEntrepreneur #PAYEJamaica
```

**Note:** #LearnOnTikTok and #BusinessTips are high-volume TikTok-native tags that boost algorithm classification. Include one high-volume general tag in every TikTok.

## Hashtag Monitoring Protocol

**Weekly:** Check which hashtags from the past week drove the most post reach (via platform analytics)

**Monthly:**
- Identify which Jamaica-specific hashtags are growing in usage
- Check if any competitors are using our primary hashtags
- Add any new relevant hashtags (regulatory announcements, new legislation)

**Quarterly:**
- Full hashtag audit — remove underperformers, add new ones
- Identify opportunity hashtags (new Jamaica business groups/communities forming)
- Cross-reference with content calendar topics

## Hashtag Blacklist (Never Use)
These create wrong associations or are off-brand:

| Hashtag | Why Not |
|---------|---------|
| #Caribbean (generic) | Too broad — doesn't target our audience |
| #Taxes (US-focused) | American audience, wrong market |
| #HR (too generic) | Diluted with global content unrelated to Jamaica |
| #Accountant (personal, not business) | Positions us as an accounting firm |
| Any political party hashtags | Brand risk |
| #Jamaica (tourism dominant) | Our content gets buried in tourism content |
