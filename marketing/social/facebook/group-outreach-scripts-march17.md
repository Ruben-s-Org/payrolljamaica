> Quick clarification for anyone running payroll in Jamaica:
>
> **NHT (National Housing Trust) — what you actually owe:**
> - Employer: 3% of *gross emoluments* (wages + regular overtime + allowances)

> - Combined: 5% of payroll going to NHT monthly
>
> **The mistake:** Many small businesses calculate NHT on base salary only and miss overtime/allowances. TAJ has been specifically auditing this.
