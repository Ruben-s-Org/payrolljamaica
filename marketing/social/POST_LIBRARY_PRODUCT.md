## POST P7: Product Feature — Employer Cost Calculation

### LINKEDIN
Most payroll calculators show you what the employee takes home.

Ours also shows you what the employee actually costs.

When you pay an employee J$200,000/month gross, your employer obligations don't stop at J$200,000:

Employer NIS: ~J$6,000 (3% up to insurable ceiling)
Employer NHT: J$6,000 (3% of J$200,000)
Employer Education Tax: ~J$6,579 (3.5% of statutory income ~J$188,000)

Total employer cost above gross salary: approximately J$18,579/month

Over a year: J$222,948 in employer-side statutory contributions — in addition to the J$2,400,000 gross salary.

For a team of 20 employees at that salary level: over J$4.4M in annual employer-side statutory contributions.

This number matters for budgeting, pricing, and hiring decisions.
