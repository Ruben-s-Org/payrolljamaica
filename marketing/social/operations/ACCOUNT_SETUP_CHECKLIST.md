**Post 2 — Educational (Day 2)**
```
Quick Jamaica payroll fact that surprises most business owners:

NIS, NHT, Education Tax, and PAYE are all calculated on different bases with different rate structures.

Get one wrong and you're either underpaying (TAJ penalty territory) or overpaying (unhappy employees).

Here's the correct order of operations for Jamaica statutory deductions:
1. Gross pay
2. Less NIS employee contribution (3% up to income ceiling)
3. Less NHT employee contribution (2%)
4. Education Tax (2.25%)
5. Income Tax (PAYE) — calculated on the remainder after above deductions and personal allowance

Most spreadsheets people use get step 5 wrong.

Our free calculator does all 5 correctly. Try it: payrolljamaica.com/calculator
```
