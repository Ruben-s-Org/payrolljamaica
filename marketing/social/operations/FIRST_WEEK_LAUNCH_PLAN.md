## ESCALATION PROTOCOL — When Things Go Wrong

**If the calculator goes down during launch week:**
1. Immediately post on all platforms: "We're aware of a technical issue and are fixing it now. We'll update shortly."
2. Fix it. Test it. Post: "We're back up — sorry for the interruption."
3. Check if anyone complained publicly — respond directly to them.

**If we get no traction on Day 1:**
Don't panic. Re-examine: Did the groups approve your posts? Did the scheduled posts actually go live? Are the links working? Fix the mechanics before changing the strategy.

**If we get media interest:**
Respond within 2 hours. Provide everything they ask for: quotes, product screenshots, user data, headshot, logo, background. This is top priority — drop everything else.

**If we get a viral complaint:**
See Crisis Comms section in COMMUNITY_MANAGEMENT_PLAYBOOK.md. Respond within 1 hour. Never delete public complaints.
