## Template 01 — LinkedIn Carousel (5 Slides): Educational Content

**Purpose:** Teach something valuable. Establish PayrollJamaica as the authority on Jamaica payroll compliance.

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px (square) — LinkedIn carousel format

**Slide-by-Slide Breakdown:**

### Slide 1 — Cover / Hook
- **Background:** Deep Forest (`#1A3A2A`) full bleed
- **Layout:** Centered, vertical stack
- **Elements:**
  - Top: Small tag label in Solar Gold (`#F2A900`) rounded rectangle — e.g., "PAYROLL GUIDE"
  - Center: Headline in Sora ExtraBold, white, 56pt — e.g., **"5 Jamaica payroll mistakes that cost you money"**
  - Bottom strip: Thin Solar Gold horizontal rule (full width, 3px) with "payrolljamaica.com" in DM Mono, white, 14pt to the right
  - Bottom-right: PayrollJamaica logomark in white, 60×60px
- **Swipe cue:** Arrow icon (chevron right) in Solar Gold, bottom-right corner, 32px

### Slides 2–4 — Content Slides (identical structure, different content)
- **Background:** Slate White (`#F7F8F6`)
- **Layout:** Left-aligned content, with a Solar Gold left border bar (8px wide, full height)
- **Elements:**
  - Top-left: Slide number in DM Mono, Deep Forest, 16pt — "02 / 05"
  - Headline: Sora Bold, Near Black, 40pt — e.g., **"Mistake #1: Wrong NIS calculation"**
  - Body: DM Sans Regular, Ash (`#8A8A8A`), 20pt, max 3 lines — explanation
  - Callout box: Amber Light (`#FFF3CC`) rectangle, rounded 12px, with the fix/tip in DM Sans SemiBold, Near Black
  - Bottom-right: Logo watermark at 40% opacity
- **Spacing:** 64px margins on all sides

### Slide 5 — CTA Slide
- **Background:** Solar Gold (`#F2A900`) full bleed
- **Layout:** Centered
- **Elements:**
  - Headline: Sora ExtraBold, Deep Forest, 52pt — **"Stop doing payroll the hard way."**
  - Subheading: DM Sans Medium, Deep Forest, 24pt — "Try PayrollJamaica free for 30 days"
  - CTA button shape: Deep Forest rounded rectangle, "payrolljamaica.com" in white, Sora Bold, 20pt
  - Bottom: Logo in Deep Forest

**Example Content Set:**
- Slide 1 hook: "5 PAYE mistakes Jamaican businesses make (and how to fix them)"
- Slide 2: Wrong employee classification (contract vs. permanent)
- Slide 3: Missing the NIS 3% employer contribution
- Slide 4: Not applying the correct income tax threshold
- Slide 5: CTA

## Template 02 — Instagram Carousel (10 Slides): Step-by-Step Payroll Guide

**Purpose:** Walk followers through a complete process. Save and share content.

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px

**Overall Style:** Clean, numbered, high-contrast. Each slide functions as a standalone lesson.

**Slide Structure:**

### Slide 1 — Cover
- **Background:** Deep Forest with dot-grid texture overlay at 5% opacity
- **Top badge:** "STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE" — Solar Gold tag, Sora Bold, 14pt
- **Title:** Sora ExtraBold, white, 52pt — **"How to run payroll in Jamaica — the complete guide"**
- **Visual:** Simplified payslip icon or calculator illustration, right side, Muted Sage line art
- **Bottom:** Solar Gold rule + "PayrollJamaica.com" label

### Slides 2–9 — Step Slides
- **Background alternates:** Odd slides = Slate White; Even slides = Amber Light (`#FFF3CC`)
- **Step number:** Huge Sora ExtraBold, Solar Gold, 120pt, top-left, behind other content at 15% opacity (decorative)
- **Step title:** Sora Bold, Deep Forest, 36pt — sits on top of the number
- **Body:** DM Sans Regular, Near Black, 18pt, max 40 words
- **Icon:** Phosphor-style icon in Deep Forest, top-right, 64×64px
- **Progress bar:** Bottom of slide — thin Muted Sage line, with Solar Gold fill showing progress (e.g., Step 3 = 30% filled)

**Example Steps:**
1. Collect employee details and tax registration info
2. Determine gross salary and employment type
3. Calculate NIS contribution (employee: 3%, employer: 3%)
4. Calculate NHT contributions (employee: 2%, employer: 3%)
5. Apply PAYE income tax based on annual threshold
6. Calculate Education Tax (employer: 3.5%, employee: 2.25%)
7. Calculate Net Pay
8. Generate payslips for each employee
9. File monthly returns with Tax Administration Jamaica

### Slide 10 — Save Prompt / CTA
- **Background:** Steel Blue (`#2D6A8F`)
- **Text:** "Save this guide for next payroll run" — Sora Bold, white, 40pt
- **CTA:** "Or let us handle it → payrolljamaica.com"
- **Icon:** Bookmark icon in Solar Gold

## Template 05 — Facebook Post: Deadline Reminder

**Purpose:** Drive urgency around filing deadlines and tax dates. Shares well in Jamaican business Facebook groups.

**Dimensions:** 1200 × 630px (Facebook link post / landscape)

**Layout:** Left content zone (65%), right graphic zone (35%)

**Left Zone (Deep Forest background):**
- Top: "DEADLINE ALERT" tag in Solar Gold, Sora Bold, 13pt
- Main date: Sora ExtraBold, Solar Gold, 72pt — e.g., **"March 14"**
- Subtitle: DM Sans Medium, white, 24pt — "Monthly PAYE return due to TAJ"
- Body: DM Sans Regular, Ash, 16pt, 2 lines max — "File on time to avoid penalties. PayrollJamaica auto-generates your return."
- CTA: Solar Gold text button — "Remind me → payrolljamaica.com"

**Right Zone (Amber Light `#FFF3CC` background):**
- Large calendar icon in Deep Forest, 160×160px, centered
- Small "TAJ" or "PAYE" label below icon in Muted Sage

**Divider:** Thin Solar Gold vertical rule between the two zones

**Key deadlines to build variations for:**
- 14th of each month: Monthly PAYE and NIS returns
- March 31: Final returns before new tax year
- April 1: New tax year begins — threshold updates
- September 15: Mid-year reminders

## Template 08 — Quote Card: Jamaican Business Wisdom

**Purpose:** Cultural resonance + shareability. Humanizes the brand.

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px

**Style:** Editorial, warm, confident. Think New York Times pull quote energy, but Jamaican.

**Layout:**

**Background:** Amber Light (`#FFF3CC`) full bleed

**Elements:**
- **Top-left decorative quotation mark:** Sora ExtraBold, Solar Gold, 200pt, positioned at -20px top / -10px left (partially off-canvas for visual drama)

## Template 10 — "Did You Know?" Card

**Purpose:** Quick-hit education. Ideal for batch creation — one card per fact, infinite supply.

**Dimensions:** 1080 × 1080px

**Layout:** Bold, single-focus, high-contrast. Immediate comprehension.

**Zones:**

**Background:** Two-zone split
- **Top 35%:** Deep Forest
- **Bottom 65%:** Slate White

**Top zone:**
- "DID YOU KNOW?" — Sora Bold, Solar Gold, 20pt, centered, letter-spaced +40
- Decorative element: 3 small Solar Gold dots (12px each) below the text

**Center (spanning both zones — this is the visual anchor):**
- Oversized icon or emoji-style illustration in a white circle (160px diameter, Deep Forest border 3px) — e.g., calendar icon for dates, calculator for rates
- The circle sits on the zone boundary, 50% in Deep Forest, 50% in Slate White

**Bottom zone:**
- Fact headline: Sora ExtraBold, Near Black, 38pt, centered — e.g., **"Employers must file PAYE returns by the 14th of each month"**
- Context line: DM Sans Regular, Ash, 17pt, centered — "Late filing attracts a penalty of 10% + interest from TAJ"
- Source/CTA: DM Mono, Muted Sage, 13pt — "Learn more: payrolljamaica.com"
- Bottom-right: Small logo watermark

**Batch content ideas:**
- "NIS was established in 1966 to provide social security for Jamaican workers"
- "The Jamaica tax year runs April 1 to March 31 — not January to December"
- "Employers are legally required to issue payslips to every employee"
- "Self-employed individuals must also pay NHT contributions"
- "PAYE stands for Pay As You Earn — tax deducted at source every pay period"
