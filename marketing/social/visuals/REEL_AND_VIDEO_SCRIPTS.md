## General Video Production Guidelines

**Equipment (minimum viable):**
- iPhone 14+ in landscape or portrait depending on format
- Clean background (plain wall, branded backdrop, or blurred office)
- Good natural light or a ring light — no harsh shadows on face
- External lapel mic for voiceover quality (Rode Wireless GO or similar)

**Captions:**
- Always add captions. 85% of social video is watched without sound.
- Use Canva, CapCut, or Descript for auto-captioning
- Caption font: match brand where possible — bold, white text with Deep Forest or black background

**Hooks that work for Jamaica payroll content:**
- Any number (time saved, money, percentages)
- "Did you know" — immediately registers as educational
- Direct challenge: "If you're still using Excel..."
- Urgency: "Before April 1..."
- Personal/confessional: "I used to dread the 14th"

**Post timing for video:**
- Instagram Reels: Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9am and 6–8pm Jamaica time
- LinkedIn: Tuesday–Wednesday, 7–9am Jamaica time (business hours, desk-browsing time)
- Facebook: Wednesday–Friday, 11am–1pm Jamaica time

**Always end with a single, clear CTA.** The worst outcome is a viewer watches the whole video and doesn't know what to do next. "Try it free at PayrollJamaica.com" is the default. Never end without it.
