## REEL 01 — "How to Calculate PAYE in Jamaica"
**Length:** 45 seconds | **Format:** Text-overlay talking head OR animated text slides
**Hook target:** HR managers, business owners doing payroll manually
**CTA:** Try the free calculator

---

### PRODUCTION NOTES
- Shoot in portrait (9:16). Clean background — white wall or branded card.
- Text overlays are mandatory (60% of viewers watch without sound).
- Pace: fast cuts every 3–4 seconds. No lingering.
- Captions: bold white text, black outline. Numbers in Solar Gold (#F4C430).
- Suggested trending audio: any mid-tempo instrumental (avoid vocals that compete with voiceover).

**[0:00–0:03] — HOOK**
*On-screen text (bold, centered):* `"Most Jamaican businesses calculate PAYE wrong."`
*Voiceover:* "Most Jamaican businesses calculate PAYE wrong — and they don't know it."

*On-screen text (appears one line at a time, each ~2 seconds):*
```

Step 2: Minus NIS (3%) → −$3,600
Step 3: Minus NHT (2%) → −$2,400
```

*Voiceover:* "The correct way: subtract NIS, NHT, and Education Tax first. Then subtract Jamaica's income threshold. PAYE applies to what's left — not the gross."

**[0:22–0:32] — PROOF / WHY IT MATTERS**
*On-screen text:* `"Same employee. Two methods. $8,000/month difference."`
*Voiceover:* "Get it wrong and your employees are overtaxed. TAJ will find the error eventually — and the correction bill falls on you."

*Voiceover:* "We built a free calculator that does all six steps correctly. Link in bio. Takes 60 seconds."

**[0:42–0:45] — LOGO CLOSE**
*On-screen text:* `PayrollJamaica | payrolljamaica.com`
