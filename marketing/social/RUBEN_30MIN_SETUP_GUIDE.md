## FAQ

**"LinkedIn asks me to verify the company — what do I do?"**
Use payrolljamaica.com as the company website. If it asks for a work email, use info@payrolljamaica.com.

**"Instagram won't let me claim @payrolljamaica — it's taken"**
Try `payrolljamaicareal`, `payrolljamaicaofficial`, or `payrolljm`. Comment on PAY-10 with what you used.

**"Facebook asks for a phone number"**
Use your personal/business number. You can hide it from the public page after setup.

**"Do I need to pay for anything?"**
No. All basic business pages/accounts are free. Don't sign up for LinkedIn Premium, Meta Business Suite paid plans, or Twitter Blue — we don't need them now.
