# PayrollJamaica — Accounting Firm Partner Programme

---

## The Problem Your Clients Face

Every Jamaican employer must calculate and remit PAYE, NHT, NIS, and Education Tax correctly — every month, every year. Most small and mid-size businesses are doing this in Excel or using software that wasn't built for Jamaica. The result: incorrect deductions, late filings, TAJ assessments, and compliance headaches that land on your desk.

**March 31 is the annual deadline.** SO1 returns, NHT annual returns, and PAYE reconciliations are all due. Your clients need this solved now.

---

## What PayrollJamaica Does

PayrollJamaica is payroll software built from the ground up for Jamaica's statutory requirements.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **PAYE** | Automatic calculation — 25% up to J$6M, 30% above, with J$1,500,096 annual threshold |
| **NHT** | 2% employee + 3% employer, no ceiling |
| **NIS** | 3% employee + 3% employer, J$5M insurable wage ceiling |
| **Education Tax** | 2.25% employee + 3.5% employer |
| **HEART/NSTA** | 3% employer contribution |
| **SO1 Returns** | Generated automatically — ready for TAJ filing |
| **NHT Annual Return** | Pre-populated from monthly payroll data |
| **Payslips** | Professional, branded, emailed to employees automatically |
| **QuickBooks / Xero** | Journal entries sync directly to your accounting software |

**Pricing:** Starts at J$3,500/month per client business. Scales with employee count.
