# Accounting Firm Outreach — Email Sequence

**Owner:** CMO
**Target:** 50 Kingston accounting firms
**Goal:** 10 partner sign-ups from first 50 outreach emails (20% conversion target)
**Timeline:** First 50 emails out by March 14 — March 31 urgency drives response

---

## Email Sequence Overview

### Email 1 — Initial Outreach (Send Day 1)

Subject options (A/B test):
- `Partnership opportunity: give your clients better Jamaica payroll (and earn 15% recurring)`
- `Are your clients calculating PAYE correctly? We can help — and pay you for it`
- `[Firm Name] × PayrollJamaica: 15% recurring commission on payroll referrals`

Use the template from `marketing/ACCOUNTING_FIRM_PARTNERSHIP_PROGRAM.md` (Accounting Firms section).

### Email 2 — Follow-up (Send Day 5 if no response)

Subject: `Re: PayrollJamaica partnership — quick follow-up`

Body:
```
Hi [Name],

Wanted to follow up on my note from earlier this week.

With March 31 three weeks away, we're seeing a lot of Jamaican businesses scrambling to reconcile payroll records. A few of them have mentioned their accountant doesn't have a tool built specifically for Jamaica's statutory requirements.

If that's a pain point for any of your clients, I'd love to show you how we handle it — and how the referral programme works.

15 minutes on a call this week?

Best,
PayrollJamaica Partnership Team
partnerships@payrolljamaica.com
```

### Email 3 — Final touch (Send Day 10 if no response)

Subject: `Last note — PayrollJamaica partner programme`

Body:
```
Hi [Name],

One last note. I understand Q4 (fiscal year-end) is a busy time for your firm.

If the partnership timing isn't right now, I'd love to reconnect after March 31. Many accounting firms find the post-deadline period is when clients start asking "how do I make sure this doesn't happen again?"

We'll be here when you're ready.

In the meantime, your clients can try our free payroll calculator at payrolljamaica.com/calculator — no sign-up required.

Best,
PayrollJamaica Partnership Team
```

## Personalization Variables

For the initial email, personalize:
1. **[Name]** — contact's first name
2. **[Firm Name]** — accounting firm name
3. **[Size-specific line]** — adjust for firm size:
   - Small firm (1-5 staff): "I know small firms need tools that work without a full implementation project..."
   - Medium firm (5-20 staff): "For a firm your size, the reseller model may actually be the better fit..."
   - Large firm (20+ staff): "Given the scope of your practice, a reseller partnership makes more sense..."

---

## Prioritization Tiers

### Tier 1 (Send first — highest probability)
- ICAJ member firms
- Firms explicitly advertising payroll services on their website
- Firms in Kingston CBD (New Kingston, Half Way Tree, Cross Roads)

### Tier 2 (Send second)
- General accounting practices in Kingston
- Firms with 5-20 staff (enough volume to make reseller worthwhile)

### Tier 3 (Send after Tier 1+2 responses are in)
- Independent bookkeepers
- Montego Bay and Portmore firms
- Very small sole-practice accountants

## Tracking — What to Record

After each email sent, update `OUTREACH_TRACKER.csv`:
- Email Variant Used (A/B test tracking)
- Outreach Date
- Response Date and type (interested / not interested / requested call / no response)
- Follow-up dates

**Target metrics:**
- Open rate: 40%+ (warm B2B Jamaica market, local sender)
- Response rate: 15-25%
- Conversion to partner sign-up: 20% of responders
- Goal: 10 new partners from first 50 emails
