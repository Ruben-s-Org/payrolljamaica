## Email 1 — Introduction + March 31 Urgency Hook

**Send:** Day 0
**Goal:** Get a reply or a demo booking. Lead with the deadline, not the product.

### Subject Line Options (A/B test)

| Option | Style | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| `March 31 is [X] days away — are your clients' payroll numbers ready?` | Urgency + question | General accounting firms |
| `[Firm Name]: earn 15% recurring on every payroll client you refer` | Direct revenue hook | Smaller firms, bookkeepers |
| `Jamaica payroll compliance tool + partner revenue for [Firm Name]` | Value + personalization | Mid-size firms |

### Body

```
Subject: March 31 is [X] days away — are your clients' payroll numbers ready?

Hi [First Name],

With Jamaica's fiscal year closing on March 31, your clients are reconciling payroll right now — and many are discovering errors. Wrong PAYE threshold. Missed NHT contributions. NIS calculated on the wrong base. These surface as TAJ assessment problems that reflect on your firm.

- NHT (2% employee / 3% employer)
- NIS (3%/3%, with J$5M insurable wage ceiling)
- Education Tax (2.25% employee / 3.5% employer)
- SO1 and NHT annual return preparation

For your firm, I'd like to propose a partnership:

→ Refer clients who subscribe — earn 15% of their subscription for 12 months

Would 15 minutes this week work for a quick demo? I'll show you the platform and the accountant dashboard — and you can decide if it's worth recommending to clients.

In the meantime, your clients can verify their numbers using our free calculator: payrolljamaica.com/calculator (no sign-up required).
```

## Personalization Variables

Replace these in every email before sending:

| Variable | Where to Find |
|---|---|
| `[First Name]` | Firm website "About" page, LinkedIn, or use "Hi" with no name |
| `[Firm Name]` | From target list |
| `[X] days away` | Calculate from send date to March 31 |
| `[X] accounting firm partners` | Current active partner count (use "several" if <5) |

### Tone Adjustments by Firm Size

| Firm Type | Adjustment |
|---|---|
| **Big 4 / Big 6** | Lead with "your SME advisory practice" — position as a tool for their clients, not competition to their services |
| **Mid-size (5-20 staff)** | Emphasize the reseller model and recurring revenue — these firms think in terms of practice growth |
| **Small / sole practitioner** | Emphasize simplicity: "no implementation project, no training required" — they need tools that just work |
| **Hospitality-focused (MoBay)** | Add: "service charge allocation, tip pooling, seasonal staff" — see `MONTEGO_BAY_OUTREACH_CAMPAIGN.md` templates |
