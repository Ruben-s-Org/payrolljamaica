## Why Accounting Firms Are Our #1 Referral Channel

Jamaica has approximately 800–1,200 registered accounting firms and thousands of independent bookkeepers. Every single one of them has clients who need payroll processed. Many handle payroll manually in Excel — charging clients J$15,000–J$50,000 per month for a labour-intensive service that creates liability for the accountant if statutory deadlines are missed.

By partnering with accounting firms, we:
1. Get warm referrals to business clients who already trust their accountant's recommendations
2. Offer accountants a better tool that reduces their risk and workload
3. Create a recurring referral revenue stream for the partner
4. Establish PayrollJamaica as the tool that "real" Jamaica accountants trust

**Target: 50 active accounting firm partners by June 2026**

---

## Partner Program Structure

### Tier 1: Referral Partner (No cost, passive income)
- Accountant refers clients to PayrollJamaica via unique referral link
- Earns **15% commission on first 12 months** of referred client subscription
- Access to white-label marketing materials
- Co-branded email template to send to clients
- Recognition on PayrollJamaica website as "Trusted Partner"

### Tier 2: Reseller Partner (Preferred for firms managing multiple clients' payroll)
- Accountant manages payroll for their clients via PayrollJamaica
- Earns **25% recurring commission** for as long as client remains active
- Master account dashboard to manage multiple client payrolls
- Priority support line (direct phone, not ticket queue)
- Quarterly business reviews with PayrollJamaica team
- Featured placement in PayrollJamaica's "Find an Accountant" directory

### Tier 3: Integration Partner (For firms using specific accounting software)
- Custom QuickBooks / Xero integration walkthrough
- Co-developed content (blog posts, webinars) on Jamaica payroll compliance
- Speaking opportunities at PayrollJamaica events/webinars
