## Tracking & Conversion Setup

### Required before launch:

1. **Google Ads conversion tracking pixel** on:
   - Signup completion page (`/signup/success` or equivalent)
   - Free trial activation
   - Contact form submission
   - Calculator email capture (micro-conversion)

2. **GA4 integration** (already set up per `analytics/ga4-setup.md`):
   - Link Google Ads to GA4
   - Import GA4 conversions into Google Ads
   - Enable auto-tagging (gclid)

3. **Remarketing audiences** to build from day one:
   - All site visitors (30 days)
   - Calculator users who did not sign up
   - Blog readers (7+ page views)
   - Pricing page visitors who did not convert
   - Signup page abandoners

4. **UTM parameters** — ensure all ad URLs use:
   - `utm_source=google`
   - `utm_medium=cpc`
   - `utm_campaign={campaign_name}`
   - `utm_term={keyword}`
   - `utm_content={ad_group}`
