### Q3: "How do I calculate PAYE?" — Shortcut: /paye

```
PAYE in Jamaica works like this:

- Annual threshold: J$1,500,096 (tax-free)
- 25% on income up to J$6,000,000
- 30% on income above J$6,000,000

Example: Employee earning J$200,000/month
Monthly threshold: J$125,008
Taxable: J$74,992
PAYE: J$74,992 x 25% = J$18,748

Calculate any salary instantly:
payrolljamaica.com/calculator
```

### Q5: "What is the March 31 deadline?" — Shortcut: /deadline

```
March 31 is Jamaica's fiscal year-end. Key deadlines:

March 31: Tax year closes. All payroll records must be reconciled.
SO1 Returns: Annual return for every employee — due to TAJ.
NHT Annual Return: Summary of all NHT contributions.
NIS Reconciliation: Verify all monthly NIS filings.

Late filing penalty: 25% of tax owed + 20% interest/year.

Full breakdown: payrolljamaica.com/march-31-payroll-deadline-jamaica
```
