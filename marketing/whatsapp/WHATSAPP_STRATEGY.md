## 3. Broadcast List Strategy

### List Segmentation

| List Name | Audience | Size Target | Message Frequency |
|-----------|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Hot Leads** | Demo requesters, calculator users who contacted us | 50-200 | 2x/week |
| **Accountants & Tax Pros** | Accounting firms, bookkeepers, tax preparers | 100-300 | 1x/week |
| **HR Managers** | HR professionals at Jamaican companies | 100-500 | 1x/week |
| **Business Owners** | SME owners, entrepreneurs | 200-1000 | 1x/week |
| **Partners** | Referral partners, ICAJ members, chamber contacts | 50-100 | 2x/month |
| **Dormant Leads** | Old inquiries, event contacts | 100-500 | 1x/month (re-engagement) |

### Building the Lists
1. **Website opt-in:** Add WhatsApp opt-in checkbox to /calculator results page and /contact form
2. **wa.me link everywhere:** Use `https://wa.me/1876XXXXXXX?text=Hi%20PayrollJamaica` on all pages
3. **QR code:** Generate WhatsApp QR code for business cards, events, PDF resources
4. **Accounting firm outreach:** When firms reply to email outreach, add to Accountants list
5. **Facebook groups:** When engaging in Jamaica business Facebook groups, direct to WhatsApp
6. **Event networking:** Every Chamber of Commerce or ICAJ event, collect WhatsApp contacts

### Broadcast Rules
- NEVER broadcast more than 3x/week to any single list
- Always include opt-out language: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- Send between 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM Jamaica time (best open rates)
- Tuesday and Thursday have highest engagement in Jamaica
- Include a clear CTA in every single message
- Keep under 300 characters when possible (WhatsApp truncates long messages in notifications)

## 4. WhatsApp Status Content Plan

WhatsApp Status mirrors the social media calendar themes but in a more casual, personal format.

### Weekly Status Schedule (Mirrors Social Calendar)

| Day | Status Type | Content |
|-----|------------|---------|
| **Monday** | Compliance Tip | Quick tax/payroll fact with countdown to March 31 |
| **Tuesday** | Calculator Highlight | Screenshot of calculator in action, link in bio |
| **Wednesday** | Client Win / Testimonial | Quote or result from a PayrollJamaica user |
| **Thursday** | Educational Carousel | 3-4 image slides explaining a payroll concept |
| **Friday** | Weekend Reminder | Light, relatable payroll meme or patois quip |

### Status Best Practices
- Post 2-3 statuses per day (they disappear in 24hrs)
- Use green/gold brand colors consistently
- Include text overlay on images (many view without sound)
- First status of the day should always be the most important (highest views)
- Use "Swipe up" or "DM us" CTAs (no clickable links in Status)

### March Urgency Escalation in Status
- **Mar 13-15:** "20 days left" — educational tone
- **Mar 16-20:** "Less than 2 weeks" — check-your-numbers tone
- **Mar 21-25:** "NEXT WEEK" — final countdown tone
- **Mar 26-31:** "DAYS LEFT" — urgent, red alert visuals

## 5. Community Group: "Jamaica Payroll & HR Community"

### Group Setup
- **Name:** Jamaica Payroll & HR Community
- **Description:** "Free community for Jamaica HR managers, payroll officers, accountants & business owners. Share tips, ask questions, stay compliant. Powered by PayrollJamaica.com"
- **Icon:** PayrollJamaica logo with community badge
- **Type:** WhatsApp Community (supports sub-groups + announcements channel)

### Community Structure
```
Jamaica Payroll & HR Community (Announcement Channel)
  |
  |-- General Discussion
  |-- Tax & Compliance Updates
  |-- HR Best Practices
  |-- Job Board (HR/Payroll Roles)
  |-- PayrollJamaica Product Updates
```

### Community Rules (Pinned)
1. Respect all members — no personal attacks
2. No spam or unsolicited sales pitches
3. Share knowledge freely — we all benefit
4. Keep discussions relevant to Jamaica payroll, HR, tax
5. Admins will share weekly compliance updates
6. Questions welcome — no such thing as a dumb payroll question

### Content Strategy for Community
- **Monday:** Weekly compliance update post (admin)
- **Wednesday:** Discussion prompt — "What's your biggest payroll challenge this week?"
- **Friday:** Resource share — link to a blog post, calculator, or downloadable
- **Ad hoc:** Break tax news immediately when TAJ announces changes
- **Monthly:** Live Q&A session with PayrollJamaica team (voice note or text-based AMA)

### Growth Tactics
1. Invite all broadcast list contacts to join
2. Promote in Facebook groups: "Join our free WhatsApp community for Jamaica payroll pros"
3. Add community join link to email signatures
4. Mention in every accounting firm meeting
5. Cross-promote with ICAJ if partnership lands
6. Target: 200 members by end of April, 500 by July

### Moderation
- Minimum 2 admins active daily
- Remove spam within 1 hour
- Welcome every new member personally (first 100 — builds culture)
- Use polls to drive engagement (WhatsApp now supports polls)
