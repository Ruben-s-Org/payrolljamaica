**Preview text:** Check these 8 items before the Jamaica tax year closes.

---

Hi [First Name],

**12 days until Jamaica's tax year closes.**

Here's your 8-point checklist to close the year cleanly:

**Before you file:**
- [ ] Pull all 12 monthly remittance receipts from TAJ, NHT, and NIS
- [ ] Verify no months were missed or late-paid
- [ ] Check NIS ceiling: employees earning over $5M/year hit the contribution ceiling — confirm you stopped deducting at the right point
- [ ] Check PAYE threshold: the 2025/26 income tax threshold is $1,500,096/year ($125,008/month). Any employee under this threshold should have no PAYE

**When you file:**
- [ ] SO1 Annual Return: file at any TAJ office or via eTAJ portal (etaj.gov.jm)
- [ ] NHT Annual Return: file at nht.gov.jm
- [ ] NIS Annual Reconciliation: submit to your NIS branch
- [ ] Issue P6 forms to any employee who requests one

**Common errors TAJ looks for:**
- PAYE applied below the income tax threshold
- Education Tax omitted or wrong rate (employee: 2.25%, employer: 3.5%)
- NIS contributions exceeding the ceiling (employer still deducting after $5M)
