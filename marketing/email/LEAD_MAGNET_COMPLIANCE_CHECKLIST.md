## Before Your First Payroll Run

**Employer Registration (TAJ)**
- [ ] Register as an employer with the Tax Administration Jamaica (TAJ)
- [ ] Obtain Employer Taxpayer Registration Number (TRN)
- [ ] Confirm PAYE withholding account is active
- [ ] Verify registration at etax.jamaicatax.gov.jm

**NHT Registration**
- [ ] Register as an employer with the National Housing Trust
- [ ] Obtain Employer NHT Number
- [ ] Collect NHT number from every employee

**NIS Registration**
- [ ] Register with the National Insurance Scheme
- [ ] Obtain Employer NIS Number
- [ ] Collect NIS number from every employee

**Per-Employee Setup**
- [ ] Collect TRN for every employee
- [ ] Collect NHT number for every employee
- [ ] Collect NIS number for every employee
- [ ] Confirm employment terms and contract type (permanent/contract/casual)
- [ ] Record official start date (for leave and redundancy purposes)

## 2025/2026 Statutory Deduction Rates

| Deduction | Employee | Employer |
|---|---|---|
| PAYE | Graduated — see below | — |
| NIS | 3% of gross wages | 3% of gross wages |
| NHT | 2% of gross wages | 3% of gross wages |
| Education Tax | 2.25% of gross wages | 3.5% of gross wages |
| HEART/NSTA | — | 3% of gross wages |

**PAYE Rates:**
- Annual income ≤ J$1,500,096: 0% (PAYE threshold — no income tax)
- Annual income J$1,500,097 – J$6,000,000: 25%
- Annual income > J$6,000,000: 30%

**Monthly threshold:** J$125,008/month (income below this = no PAYE)

*Use the free calculator at payrolljamaica.com/calculator to verify deductions.*

## Monthly Payroll Checklist

Run this every month before the 14th:

**By Payroll Run Date**
- [ ] Collect all timesheets / attendance records
- [ ] Record any salary changes, new hires, or terminations
- [ ] Calculate gross pay for each employee
- [ ] Calculate all statutory deductions (PAYE, NIS, NHT, Education Tax)
- [ ] Calculate employer contributions
- [ ] Generate payslips for each employee
- [ ] Issue payslips to employees by pay date

**By the 14th of Each Month (TAJ Deadlines)**
- [ ] File S01 Monthly Return on eTax (Jamaica Tax Portal)
- [ ] Remit PAYE, NIS, NHT, and Education Tax to TAJ
- [ ] File NHT Monthly Schedule online
- [ ] Remit NHT contributions to NHT

*Late filing attracts penalty + interest. No grace period.*

## March 31 Annual Deadlines

These are the most critical annual compliance deadlines:

**NHT Annual Return (Due: March 31)**
- [ ] Complete NHT S02 Annual Return
- [ ] List all employees with individual contributions for the year
- [ ] Submit online at nht.gov.jm
- [ ] Keep a copy on file for 7 years

**SO1 Annual Reconciliation**
- [ ] Reconcile all monthly S01 returns against annual payroll total
- [ ] Identify and correct any discrepancies before filing
- [ ] Confirm all PAYE remitted = total withheld from employees

**P6 Employer Certificate (Issue to Employees by March 31)**
- [ ] Generate P6 for every employee who worked during the year
- [ ] P6 shows: gross wages, PAYE withheld, NIS, NHT, Education Tax
- [ ] Employees need P6 to file personal income tax returns

---

## April 1 — New Tax Year Checklist

- [ ] Reset cumulative PAYE calculations for all employees
- [ ] Confirm statutory rates are current (check payrolljamaica.com for updates)
- [ ] Update NIS contribution ceiling if changed in March budget
- [ ] Update any salary changes effective April 1
- [ ] Confirm first S01 filing due May 14 (covering April payroll)

---

## Record Keeping Requirements

TAJ requires you to keep payroll records for **7 years**. Store the following:
- [ ] Monthly payroll runs (all employee calculations)
- [ ] All S01 returns (copy of what was filed)
- [ ] All NHT Monthly Schedules
- [ ] NHT Annual Returns
- [ ] P6 certificates issued
- [ ] Employee TRN, NIS, NHT records
- [ ] Any TAJ correspondence
