## CONTENT STRATEGY FOR FOLLOWER GROWTH

### Post types that drive follows on LinkedIn (in order)
1. **Strong opinion + data** — "Here's why Jamaica businesses overpay on payroll" → shares → new followers
2. **Educational threads** — "5 things every Jamaica employer gets wrong about PAYE" → saves → follows
3. **Founder story** — personal narrative gets 3–5x more reach than brand posts
4. **Countdown urgency** — March 31 content gets saved and shared as deadline approaches
5. **Polls** — "How do you currently run payroll in Jamaica?" → algorithm boosts, followers engage

### Posts to AVOID (won't drive follows)
- Pure promotional posts ("Buy PayrollJamaica") — people don't follow to be sold to
- Generic business content with no Jamaica-specific angle
- Reshares without Ruben's commentary
