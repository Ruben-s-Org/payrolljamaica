## MARCH 14 — LINKEDIN POST

```
Quick Jamaica payroll math lesson:

Most expensive payroll mistake in Jamaica right now:

Getting the deduction ORDER wrong.

Jamaica's statutory deduction sequence:

1️⃣ Gross Wages
2️⃣ Minus NIS (3% employee, capped at J$5M annual)
3️⃣ Minus Income Tax Threshold (J$1,500,096/year)
4️⃣ Taxable Income
5️⃣ PAYE: 25% of Taxable Income
6️⃣ NHT: 2% of Gross
7️⃣ Education Tax: 2.25% of Taxable Income

Skip step 2 before step 5 → you're overwitholding PAYE from employees.

Most generic software does this. Most old spreadsheets do this.

With 17 days until March 31, this matters.

Check your numbers: payrolljamaica.com/calculator
```
