### Canva Efficiency Tips for Week 4
1. **Build once, duplicate fast** — create Mar 25 carousel, then duplicate the template for each subsequent day's urgency posts
2. **Red variant** — save a "red background" version of every template. Swap just the countdown number
3. **The "3-click swap"** — each daily post = change the number, change the date text, change the headline. Body copy stays the template
4. **Story sets** — create all story backgrounds in one session (they share the same layout — just the center text changes)
5. **Batch export** — export all 14 assets in one session on March 24 so you're not designing during deadline week

### What NOT to design (copy-only posts — no graphic needed)
- Mar 25 LinkedIn text post (long-form, no image)
- Mar 27 Facebook evening post (link share to calculator)
- Mar 30 X/Twitter thread (text only)
- Mar 31 afternoon LinkedIn (text-only compliance update)

### Design time estimate
- ~3 hours total for all Week 4 assets (14 graphics)
- Best batch: Mar 24 (Monday) — design everything for the whole week
- Order: Start with DEADLINE DAY hero (Brief 12) while you're fresh
