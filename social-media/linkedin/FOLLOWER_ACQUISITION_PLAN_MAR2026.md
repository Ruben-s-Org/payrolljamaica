## LINKEDIN ALGORITHM NOTES — What Boosts Reach

**What the LinkedIn algorithm rewards for company pages in 2026:**
1. **Dwell time** — posts people spend time reading (educational > promotional)
2. **Early engagement** — first 60 minutes after posting determines reach
3. **Comments over likes** — comments = 5-10x higher weight than likes
4. **Polls** — get the most reach of any post type
5. **Document posts (PDFs)** — second highest reach (use for infographic content)
6. **Native video** — third highest, but high effort
7. **Regular posting** — 3-5x per week optimal for new pages

**What hurts reach:**
- Links in the post body (put link in first comment instead)
- Too many hashtags (3-5 max for company pages)
- Posting and immediately editing
- Posting more than once per day

**Pro tip for all posts this month:** Post → immediately comment on your own post with the link ("💡 Full details: payrolljamaica.com/calculator"). This keeps links out of the post body and boosts reach significantly.
