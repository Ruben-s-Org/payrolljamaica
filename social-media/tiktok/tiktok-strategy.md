## Why TikTok for PayrollJamaica

TikTok Jamaica is growing fast — particularly among 25–44 year olds who are business owners, entrepreneurs, and middle managers. The "business TikTok" (BizTok) community in the Caribbean is active and underserved. Payroll content performs well in this niche because:

- Fear of TAJ audits = high emotional engagement
- "Did you know?" compliance facts = strong shares
- Jamaican dialect + personality = virality in the local market
- The March 31 fiscal year-end is a genuine urgency hook right now
