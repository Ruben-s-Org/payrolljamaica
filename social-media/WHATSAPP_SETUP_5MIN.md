# WHATSAPP BUSINESS — 5 MINUTE SETUP
**PayrollJamaica | Do this now on your phone**
*Estimated time: 5 minutes flat*

---

## Step 1: Download (1 min)

App Store (iPhone) or Play Store (Android) → search **"WhatsApp Business"**

> Look for the green icon with a **B** in the phone handset — NOT the regular WhatsApp icon.

Install it. Open it.

---

## Step 2: Register (1 min)

Enter your phone number.

> **Which number to use?**
> - A separate business number is ideal (cleaner separation)
> - Your personal number works fine too — you can switch it later
> - If you use your personal number, your existing WhatsApp chats carry over

Verify with the SMS code. Done.

---

## Step 3: Business Profile (2 min)

Go to: **Settings → Business Profile** and fill in:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Business name** | PayrollJamaica |
| **Category** | Business Services |
| **Description** | Jamaica's fast payroll software. PAYE • NHT • NIS calculated instantly. Try free: payrolljamaica.com |
| **Website** | https://payrolljamaica.com |
| **Email** | hello@payrolljamaica.com |
| **Address** | (Optional — leave blank or add Jamaica) |

**Profile photo:** Upload the PayrollJamaica logo (green/gold square).

Hit **Save**.

## Step 4: Set Your First Status Right Now (30 sec)

Go to: **Status tab → Add Status → Text**

Copy-paste this exactly:

```

payrolljamaica.com/calculator 🇯🇲
```
