## 1. Why Google Business Profile Matters for PayrollJamaica

Google Business Profile (GBP) is free and is one of the highest-ROI local SEO actions you can take. Here's why it's critical for PayrollJamaica:

**Search behavior of the target customer:**
When a Kingston business owner needs payroll software, they search:
- "payroll software Jamaica"
- "payroll software Kingston"
- "payroll service Jamaica"
- "PAYE calculator Jamaica"
- "payroll company Kingston Jamaica"

Google prioritizes local results — businesses with a verified GBP profile and strong reviews appear in the **map pack** (the 3-business box at the top of search results). This is prime real estate that no amount of paid advertising can easily displace.

**What a complete GBP profile gets you:**
- Appears in Google Maps searches
- Shows in the "Local Pack" (top 3 local results)
- Displays your hours, phone number, website link, and reviews on the search results page — without the user even clicking your site
- Star ratings increase click-through rate by 25–30%
- Reviews build trust with Jamaican business owners who are skeptical of unfamiliar software vendors

**The competitive opportunity:** Most Jamaica-based B2B software companies have incomplete or unclaimed GBP profiles. A fully optimized, review-rich profile will outperform competitors immediately.

## 2. Step-by-Step Setup Guide

### Step 1: Create or Claim Your Profile

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with the company Google account (create one at `payrolljamaica@gmail.com` or use a Workspace account `hello@payrolljamaica.com` — Workspace is preferred for professionalism)
3. Click "Add your business to Google"
4. Search "Payroll Jamaica" — if a listing already exists, click "Claim this business" and proceed through verification. If not, click "Add your business"

### Step 2: Enter Business Name
**Exact name:** `Payroll Jamaica`

Do not add keywords to your business name (e.g., "Payroll Jamaica — Payroll Software"). This violates Google's guidelines and can get your profile suspended. Your business name should match what customers know you as.

### Step 3: Choose Business Category
**Primary Category:** `Payroll Service`

If "Payroll Service" is not available in Jamaica's category list, use:
- `Software Company` (second best option)
- `Accounting Software` (third option)
- `Business Service` (fallback)

**Add secondary categories:**
- Human Resources Consultant
- Accounting Service
- Business Management Consultant

### Step 4: Service Area Setup

PayrollJamaica is an online service — you don't have a storefront customers visit. Set up as a **service-area business**.

1. When asked "Do you want to add a location customers can visit?", select **No**
2. When asked for service area, add:
   - **Kingston, Jamaica**
   - **Saint Andrew, Jamaica**
   - **Spanish Town, Jamaica**
   - **Montego Bay, Jamaica**
   - **Portmore, Jamaica**
   - **Jamaica** (country level — to capture all island searches)

This tells Google you serve customers across Jamaica without listing a physical address.

### Step 5: Add Contact Information
- **Phone:** Your Jamaica business number (get a local Kingston number via FLOW or Digicel — essential for local credibility)
- **Website:** `https://payrolljamaica.com`
- **Email:** Not displayed publicly on GBP, but use `hello@payrolljamaica.com` for the account

### Step 6: Set Business Hours

Even as an online SaaS business, set business hours — it builds trust and affects your rank in "open now" searches.

```
Monday:     8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Tuesday:    8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Wednesday:  8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Thursday:   8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Friday:     8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Saturday:   9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Sunday:     Closed
```

Add special hours for Jamaica public holidays (the 13 public holidays) — Google provides a holiday hours prompt each year.

### Step 7: Write the Business Description

**Character limit:** 750 characters. Write for humans first, but include key search terms naturally.

**Recommended Description (copy-paste ready):**

> Payroll Jamaica is payroll software built specifically for Jamaican businesses. Calculate PAYE, NHT, NIS, and Education Tax automatically using current TAJ rates. Process payroll in minutes — not hours — with payslips, remittance reports, and deadline reminders included.
>
> Trusted by SMEs, HR managers, and accountants across Jamaica. Fully compliant with TAJ requirements, Jamaica Employment Act, and NHT/NIS regulations.
>
> Start free: payrolljamaica.com. No credit card required.

**Word count check:** This is approximately 580 characters — within the 750-character limit.

### Step 8: Verify Your Business

Google requires verification to publish your profile. Options for virtual businesses:
1. **Video verification** (most common for service-area businesses) — record a short video showing you at your workspace and on your computer using the business
2. **Phone call verification** — Google calls your business phone and reads a code
3. **Postcard verification** — Google mails a postcard to a Jamaica address (takes 5–14 days)

**Recommended:** Use phone verification with your Jamaica business number for fastest turnaround.

## 5. Q&A Optimization — Preloaded Questions and Answers

Google Business Profile has a Q&A section where anyone can ask — and anyone can answer. You should preload your own questions and answers to control the narrative and capture long-tail search queries.

**How to add preloaded Q&As:**
1. Search for "Payroll Jamaica" in Google Maps (while logged into your GBP account)
2. Scroll to the Q&A section
3. Click "Ask a question"
4. Type the question (from a regular Google account, not your GBP account — or have a trusted person do it)
5. Then log into your GBP account and answer it as the business owner

**Preloaded Q&As to Add:**

---

**Q: Does Payroll Jamaica support PAYE calculations for Jamaican businesses?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica automatically calculates PAYE using current TAJ rates and Jamaica's personal income tax threshold. Every payroll run generates the correct PAYE withholding for each employee, plus your monthly remittance figures. Learn more at payrolljamaica.com.

---

**Q: Can I use Payroll Jamaica to calculate NHT and NIS contributions?**
A: Absolutely. Payroll Jamaica calculates NHT (2% employee / 3% employer) and NIS contributions automatically for every employee on every pay run. Your monthly S01 remittance report is generated in one click.

---

**Q: Is Payroll Jamaica suitable for small businesses in Kingston?**
A: Yes — Payroll Jamaica is designed specifically for Jamaican small and medium businesses. Whether you have 1 employee or 100, the software handles full TAJ compliance, payslip generation, and remittance reporting. Businesses from Kingston to Montego Bay use it.

---

**Q: How do I register for payroll in Jamaica for the first time?**
A: First-time employers in Jamaica need to: (1) register with TAJ for a PAYE employer account, (2) register with NHT, (3) register with NIS. Payroll Jamaica guides you through this setup process and helps you process your first payroll correctly. Visit payrolljamaica.com to get started free.

---

**Q: What is the monthly payroll deadline in Jamaica?**
A: Employers must remit PAYE, NHT, NIS, and Education Tax to TAJ by the 14th of the month following the pay period. Payroll Jamaica sends automatic reminders before this deadline. Late remittances incur a 10% penalty plus interest.

---

**Q: Does Payroll Jamaica handle Jamaica statutory holiday pay?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica includes Jamaica's full public holiday calendar and automatically calculates statutory holiday pay (time-and-a-half or day in lieu) based on employee work records and Jamaica Employment Act requirements.

---

**Q: Is there a free trial for Payroll Jamaica software?**
A: Yes — Payroll Jamaica offers a free 30-day trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Start at payrolljamaica.com.

---

**Q: Does Payroll Jamaica produce payslips for employees?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica automatically generates payslips for every employee on every pay run, showing gross pay, all statutory deductions (PAYE, NHT, NIS, Education Tax), and net pay. Payslips can be emailed directly to employees or downloaded as PDFs.

## 6. Review Acquisition Strategy

Google reviews are the #1 ranking factor for local GBP placement and the #1 trust signal for new prospects. A business with 50+ reviews with an average of 4.5+ stars will outperform competitors in local searches consistently.

### Who to Ask for Reviews

**Priority 1: Trial users who completed a successful first payroll run**
These users have the highest satisfaction and recency. Trigger a review request in-app or via email 7 days after their first successful payroll processing.

**Priority 2: Paying subscribers after their first month**
After first payment, send an automated email: "You've completed your first month — we'd love to hear how it's going."

**Priority 3: Support interactions that ended positively**
If a customer contacted support and the issue was resolved, follow up within 24 hours: "We're glad we could help. If you have a moment, a Google review makes a real difference for a small Jamaica business."

### How to Ask (Templates)

**In-App Prompt (appears after successful payroll run):**
> "You've processed your first payroll — great work! Would you take 30 seconds to leave us a Google review? It helps other Jamaican businesses find us."
> [Leave a Review] button → links directly to GBP review page

**Email Template:**
> Subject: "Quick favor? (Takes 60 seconds)"
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> You've been using Payroll Jamaica for [X months] now. I hope it's been saving you time on payroll every month.
>
> If it has — would you be willing to leave us a quick Google review? It takes about 60 seconds and genuinely helps other Jamaican business owners find us.
>
> [Leave a Google Review →] [insert direct review link]
>
> No pressure at all — and thank you either way.
>
> Ruben
> Payroll Jamaica

**Get Your Direct Review Link:**
1. Search "Payroll Jamaica" in Google
2. Click your GBP listing
3. Click "Write a review"
4. Copy the URL from your browser — this is your direct review link. Share this link directly in all review requests.

### Review Response Protocol

Respond to every review within 24–48 hours:

**Positive review response template:**
> "Thank you so much, [Name]! We're really glad Payroll Jamaica is making compliance easier for your business. If you ever need anything, we're always here. — Ruben"

**Negative review response template:**
> "Thank you for the feedback, [Name]. I'm sorry your experience wasn't what it should have been. I'd like to make this right — please email us at hello@payrolljamaica.com and we'll resolve this as a priority. — Ruben"

**Never be defensive, never argue.** Future customers read your responses. A gracious response to a negative review often impresses readers more than the review itself.

### Review Volume Targets

| Timeframe | Target Reviews | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Month 1–3 | 10–15 reviews | Ask founding users directly |
| Month 3–6 | 30–50 reviews | Automate in-app + email requests |
| Month 6–12 | 75–100 reviews | Ongoing automation + support follow-ups |
| Year 2+ | 200+ reviews | Compound growth from larger user base |

## 7. Photo Guidelines

Photos significantly improve GBP profile engagement. Google recommends at minimum: logo, cover photo, and 3–5 additional photos.

### Required Photos

**1. Logo**
- Format: Square, 250x250px minimum, 1200x1200px recommended
- Use the PayrollJamaica logo on white or transparent background
- File name: `payroll-jamaica-logo.png`

**2. Cover Photo (the banner image on your profile)**
- Format: 1080x608px minimum (16:9 ratio), 2120x1192px ideal
- Show: Dashboard screenshot or branded graphic with "Jamaica Payroll Software" text
- Should look professional — this is the first visual impression

### Recommended Additional Photos

**3. Product Screenshots (3–5 images)**
- Upload high-quality screenshots of the dashboard, payslip view, PAYE calculator, and remittance report screen
- Caption each: "PAYE Calculation Dashboard", "Automatic Payslip Generator", "One-Click TAJ Remittance Report"
- These answer the "what does it look like?" question immediately

**4. Team Photo (1–2 images)**
- A photo of the founder or small team at a desk or on a video call
- Makes the business feel human and local — important for Jamaica trust signals
- Caption: "The Payroll Jamaica team — based in Jamaica, built for Jamaica"

**5. Jamaica Context Photo (1–2 images)**
- A branded graphic with a Jamaica flag or Kingston skyline element
- Or a photo taken in Jamaica (coffee shop, co-working space) with laptop visible
- Signals local presence and authenticity

### Photo Upload Tips
- Always geotag photos before uploading (add Jamaica GPS coordinates via photo metadata)
- Upload at least one new photo per month — Google rewards profile activity
- Use descriptive file names before uploading (Google reads file names): `payroll-jamaica-dashboard-screenshot.jpg` not `IMG_4823.jpg`
- Name file names with target keywords where natural
