### POST 1 — LinkedIn | "Why Jamaica payroll is different"
```
Most payroll software wasn't built for Jamaica.

Generic tools calculate PAYE before deducting NIS. That's wrong.

The correct Jamaican statutory order:

1️⃣ Gross wages
2️⃣ Minus NIS (3%, applied first)
3️⃣ Minus income tax threshold (J$1,500,096/year)
4️⃣ = Taxable income
5️⃣ PAYE: 25% of taxable income
6️⃣ NHT: 2% of gross
7️⃣ Education Tax: 2.25% of taxable income

Skip step 2 before step 5 → you're over-withholding PAYE from every employee.

Most spreadsheets and international software do exactly this.

We don't.

payrolljamaica.com/calculator — free check, no sign-up.
```
