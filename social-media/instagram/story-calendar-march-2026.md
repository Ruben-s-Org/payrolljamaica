# Instagram Story Calendar — March 2026
**Platform:** Instagram Stories (@payrolljamaica)
**Goal:** Daily Story presence (separate from feed posts) — drives profile visits, link-in-bio clicks, and follower retention

---

## How to Use This Calendar

- Stories last 24 hours — post daily even on days with no feed post
- Use **Link Sticker** (not swipe-up) to drive calculator traffic — tap the chain link icon when creating Story
- Use **Poll sticker** and **Question sticker** to drive replies (replies = Instagram DMs = warm leads)
- Post Stories between **8–10 AM Jamaica time** for maximum views (people check phones before work)
- Save important Stories as **Highlights** (categories below)

## Story Best Practices for Instagram Algorithm

| Practice | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Post time | 8–10 AM Jamaica time (peak morning scroll) |
| Frequency | Daily minimum. Twice daily for final week (Mar 26–31) |
| Interaction | Reply to every poll vote and question sticker response within 2 hours |
| Link sticker | Use on every educational Story — drives calculator traffic |
| Saves as Highlights | Every Story in a category should be saved to the relevant Highlight |
| Font size | Keep text large enough to read on a phone — test on your own phone before posting |
| Text volume | Max 3-4 lines per slide — split into multiple slides for longer content |
