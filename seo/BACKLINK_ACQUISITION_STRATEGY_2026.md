- **Target links:** 15–20 backlinks from Tier 1 & 2 sites

## 4. Link Building Tactics

### Tactic 1: Press Release & Media Outreach (Industry Report)

**Frequency:** Major push quarterly; minor updates monthly during budget/tax season

**Process:**
1. Send Industry Report press release to editors at: Jamaica Observer, Gleaner, Loop, RJR, CVM
2. Follow up with a personalised email offering an exclusive quote or interview
3. Offer to write a bylined opinion piece on payroll compliance tied to the data
4. Track which publications run the story; follow up for link inclusion if online article doesn't link

**Target:** 3–5 backlinks from this tactic in March–April 2026

---

### Tactic 2: Resource Page Link Building

**Target sites:** JBDC, PSOJ, JEF, Jamaica Chamber, JAMPRO, ICAJ, HR Jamaica

**Process:**
1. Identify each organisation's "Resources for Business" or "Tools for Members" pages
2. Email the relevant contact (webmaster, communications manager, membership director)
3. Introduce PayrollJamaica calculator and report as a free resource for their audience
4. Request inclusion on the resource page with a brief description and link
5. Offer reciprocal promotion (feature them in a PayrollJamaica newsletter or blog post)

**Template subject line:** *"Free payroll compliance resource for [ORGANISATION NAME] members"*

**Target:** 3–5 backlinks from this tactic per quarter

---

### Tactic 3: Guest Posting & Contributed Content

**Target publications:** Accountancy firm blogs, HR Jamaica sites, Caribbean business media, JBDC blog

**Topics that will get accepted:**
- "How Jamaican SMEs Can Avoid the 5 Most Common Payroll Errors"
- "What Every Jamaican Employer Needs to Know Before March 31"
- "PAYE vs. Education Tax: What's the Difference and Why It Matters"
- "The True Cost of Getting Payroll Wrong in Jamaica"
- "Jamaica's NHT: The Payroll Rule Most Businesses Get Wrong"

**Process:**
1. Identify which target sites accept guest content (most accountancy firm blogs, JBDC, Caribbean business media)
2. Send a pitch with 3 topic options and a brief bio
3. Write a 800–1,200 word piece that naturally includes 1–2 links to PayrollJamaica (calculator, report, or relevant guide page)
4. Ensure the author bio includes a link to payrolljamaica.com

**Target:** 1–2 guest posts per month = 2–4 backlinks/month from DA 25+ sites

---

### Tactic 4: HARO-Style Quote Provision (Reactive PR)

Jamaica's business journalists regularly need expert quotes for payroll/tax/SME compliance stories, particularly:
- Budget season (March–May)
- Mid-year economic review (October–November)
- Tax amnesty / compliance campaigns

**Process:**
1. Monitor Jamaica Observer, Gleaner, Loop Business, and RJR News for stories on: payroll, taxes, PAYE, SME compliance, Budget implications
2. Email the reporter directly (or via media contact form) offering a data point or quote from the Industry Report
3. Make it easy: offer a pre-written quote they can use in 30 seconds
4. Follow up after publication to ensure online article includes a link

**Monitoring tools:**
- Google Alerts: "Jamaica payroll", "PAYE Jamaica", "TAJ compliance", "Jamaica SME", "Jamaica Budget 2026"
- Social monitoring: follow Jamaica Observer, Gleaner, Loop Business on Twitter/X and LinkedIn

**Target:** 1–3 reactive backlinks/month during active news periods

### Tactic 6: Partnership Link Exchanges (White Hat)

**Identify non-competing partners who serve the same audience:**
- Jamaican HR consultancies (HRMAJ members)
- Jamaican accounting software companies (non-payroll features)
- Jamaica employment law firms
- Jamaican business formation services
- Jamaica benefits/insurance providers targeting SMEs

**Process:**
1. Reach out with a genuine value proposition: feature their service in a relevant PayrollJamaica blog post
2. In exchange, ask them to link to a relevant PayrollJamaica resource (calculator, guide, or report) from their website
3. Only pursue exchanges with DA 20+ sites and genuinely relevant audiences — do not exchange links with irrelevant or spammy sites

**Target:** 3–5 link exchange partnerships per quarter

## 6. Anchor Text Strategy

### Anchor Text Distribution (Recommended Mix)

| Anchor Type | Example | Target % |
|---|---|---|
| Branded | "PayrollJamaica", "payrolljamaica.com" | 40% |
| Partial match / natural | "Jamaica payroll software", "Jamaica payroll calculator" | 25% |
| Generic | "click here", "learn more", "this resource" | 15% |
| Naked URL | "www.payrolljamaica.com" | 10% |
| Exact match (use sparingly) | "payroll software Jamaica", "PAYE calculator Jamaica" | 10% |

**Why this mix:** Over-optimised exact-match anchor text is a Google penalty risk. A natural, branded-heavy anchor profile is both safer and more authentic for a genuine business brand.

### Anchor Text Phrases to Cultivate

**For the calculator:**
- "free Jamaica PAYE calculator"
- "Jamaica payroll calculator"
- "calculate Jamaica take-home pay"

**For the industry report:**
- "Jamaica Payroll Industry Report 2026"
- "PayrollJamaica's 2026 industry research"
- "Jamaica payroll compliance data"

**For the platform:**
- "PayrollJamaica"
- "Jamaica's payroll software"
- "cloud payroll Jamaica"
