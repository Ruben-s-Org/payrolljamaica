## Priority 3: Client Testimonials Page

**Current state:** No testimonials page. Reviews exist but are not displayed on site.
**E-E-A-T impact:** Experience and Trustworthiness (social proof from real users).
**Timeline:** Launch by May 31, 2026.

**URL:** `/testimonials` or `/customers`

### Structure

**Section 1: Featured Testimonials (3–5 video or photo testimonials)**
- Real customer, real name, real business name and industry
- Format: Photo + quote + business name + employee count + specific outcome
- Example: "Since switching to PayrollJamaica in January, we've cut our payroll processing time from 4 hours to 45 minutes and had zero TAJ compliance issues. — Sandra M., Owner, [Business Name], Kingston, 12 employees"

**Section 2: Google Review Feed**
- Embed or manually curate recent Google Business Profile reviews
- Show reviewer name, rating, date, review text, and Google attribution

**Section 3: Industry/Size Breakdown**
- Testimonials organized by: Retail | Hospitality | Professional Services | Healthcare | Construction
- Testimonials organized by size: 1–5 employees | 6–20 employees | 20+ employees

**Section 4: Results / Outcomes (Aggregate Stats)**
- "Employers using PayrollJamaica save an average of X hours per month"
- "X% of PayrollJamaica employers have zero TAJ compliance penalties"
- Only use stats you can substantiate from actual data

### Collection Process
1. Email existing customers (see Review Generation Campaign)
2. For video testimonials: offer 2 free months in exchange for a 60-second video recorded on their phone
3. For written: use the review email sequence, then request permission to feature on website
