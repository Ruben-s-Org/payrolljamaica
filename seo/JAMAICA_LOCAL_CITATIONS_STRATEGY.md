# Jamaica Local Citations Strategy — PayrollJamaica

## What Are Local Citations?
Local citations are online mentions of PayrollJamaica's name, address, and phone number (NAP) on directories, websites, and platforms. They are a key Google local ranking factor — the more consistent and widespread the citations, the higher we rank in Jamaica local search.
