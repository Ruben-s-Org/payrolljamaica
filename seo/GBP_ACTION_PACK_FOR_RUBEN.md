### STEP 5 — Trigger Verification (3 minutes)

17. Google will now show verification options. The options offered depend on your account age and business type. Common options:

    **Option A — Phone/Text (fastest — do this if available):**
    - Select "Call me" or "Text me"
    - Google calls/texts your phone with a 6-digit code
    - Enter the code immediately
    - Verification complete — profile goes live within minutes

    **Option B — Email (fast):**
    - Select "Email verification"
    - Check hello@payrolljamaica.com for a verification code
    - Enter code on screen
    - Verification complete — profile goes live within minutes

    **Option C — Postcard (slow — last resort):**

    **Option D — Video verification (if offered):**
    - Google may offer a live video call where you show your business/workspace on camera
    - Takes 5–10 minutes — faster than postcard
