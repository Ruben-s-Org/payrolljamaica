## Local SEO Signals (Jamaica-Specific)

### Geographic Modifiers to Include in Content
- "Jamaica" / "Jamaican" (primary)
- "Kingston" (capital, highest search density)
- "Montego Bay" / "MoBay" (second business hub)
- "Portmore" / "Spanish Town" / "Mandeville"
- "JA" (informal abbreviation Jamaicans use)

### Industry Verticals with High Payroll Search Volume in Jamaica
1. **Tourism & Hospitality** — hotel groups, resorts, restaurants (service charge, tip pool)
2. **Construction** — project-based workers, union deductions
3. **Retail** — part-time staff, multiple pay rates
4. **Healthcare** — shift premiums, overtime
5. **Government & Non-profit** — statutory compliance critical
6. **Small business (1-10 employees)** — largest volume segment

### Jamaica-Specific Content Angles (Not Used by Competitors)
- "TAJ compliance" and "TAJ audit" (Tax Administration Jamaica)
- "Ministry of Labour Jamaica" compliance
- "Labour Relations and Industrial Disputes Act" content
- Budget Day (March) and Tax Year-End (March 31) — high-traffic seasonal moments
- "JMD payroll" (Jamaica dollar payroll)
