## 5. GBP Q&A SECTION (pre-seed these questions + answers)

**Q: Do you handle PAYE for Jamaican businesses?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica automates PAYE calculation at current Jamaica Tax Authority rates. We stay updated with every budget announcement — your rates are always correct without manual entry.

**Q: Can I file my NIS and NHT returns using Payroll Jamaica?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica generates NIS Schedule 7 and NHT employer returns monthly. You submit directly to NIS and NHT — we provide the reports in the correct format.

**Q: Do you help with the March 31 Jamaica payroll year-end deadline?**
A: Absolutely. The March 31 fiscal year-end is the most critical compliance date for Jamaican employers. Payroll Jamaica generates your SO1 annual return, NHT annual return, and TD4 summaries automatically — all you do is review and submit.

**Q: How much does Payroll Jamaica cost?**
A: Plans start from J$2,500/month for small Jamaican businesses. Pricing is transparent and in Jamaican dollars — no USD conversion fees. Visit payrolljamaica.com for full pricing.

A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica is fully cloud-based and serves all 14 Jamaican parishes — Kingston, Montego Bay, Spanish Town, Mandeville, Ocho Rios, Negril, Port Antonio, May Pen, and everywhere across Jamaica.

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**
A: Most businesses are live within one business day. Import your employee list, set pay rates and deductions, and run your first payroll. Jamaica tax tables are pre-loaded.

**Q: Do you support the NHT annual return?**
A: Yes. Payroll Jamaica generates the NHT employer annual return required before March 31 each year. It includes all employee NHT contributions for the fiscal year in the correct NHT format.

**Q: Can small businesses (under 10 employees) use Payroll Jamaica?**
A: Yes — Payroll Jamaica is built for businesses of all sizes. Our entry plan is designed for small Jamaican businesses with 1–10 employees. The same statutory compliance as large companies, at a small business price.

**Q: Do you support the Education Tax deduction?**
A: Yes. Education Tax is automatically calculated and included in every payslip and remittance report. The 2025/2026 Education Tax rate is applied automatically.

**Q: Is Payroll Jamaica only for Kingston businesses?**
